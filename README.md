## 🚀 About Me
I'm an embedded systems engineer and hardware-first developer focused on IoT, firmware, and PCB design. I enjoy combining hardware and software to deliver practical, reliable solutions — from quick prototypes to production-ready designs.

## 💼 What I Do
- **Embedded Systems Development:** Arduino, microcontrollers, ESP32, firmware engineering
- **PCB Design & Electronics:** circuit design, board layout, and hardware prototyping
- **Software & Tooling:** Electron apps, Node.js tooling, and automation for development workflows

## 🔧 Technologies & Tools
- **Languages:** C/C++, JavaScript, TypeScript, Python
- **Embedded:** Arduino, ESP32, AVR microcontrollers, firmware
- **Design & CAD:** PCB layout, circuit design, EasyEDA, OSHWLab
- **Tools & Workflows:** Git, GitHub, VS Code, Electron, CI/CD

## 🌱 Currently Working On
- Building new Arduino & ESP32 IoT solutions
- Designing compact PCBs for low-power embedded projects
- Developing developer workflows and AI-assisted tools for embedded engineers
